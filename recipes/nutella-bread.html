Nutella Sandwich

Description

I lived nutella-bread based for some years of my adolescence

I might have chosen this recipe because it is very short and therefore i'll finish this exercise sooner

Ingredients

  • bread
  • nutella
  • freaks even add butter but I do not recommend

Steps

  1. Heat your bread. Optimum would be toast. Toast the toast
  2. If you are crazy add a layer of butter on top of the toast
  3. Smash a whole lot of nutella on top of your toast
  4. Eat
