Instant Ramen

Description

Instant Ramen is another chosen recipe for it's short contents

I might have chosen this recipe because it is very short and therefore i'll finish this exercise sooner

Ingredients

  • boiling water
  • 1 package of instant ramen
  • optional: egg

Steps

  1. Boil water with an egg
  2. add instant ramen, remove egg
  3. peel egg and put back to ramen
  4. Eat
